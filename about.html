About Me

Hi, I’m Michael - a security researcher with a background in embedded systems, vulnerability research, and reverse engineering. I’m especially interested in low-level security, firmware, proprietary protocols, and understanding how systems work under the hood.

This blog is where I share notes from my journey in cybersecurity and penetration testing, including write-ups, lessons learned, and technical experiments as I continue growing in the field.
